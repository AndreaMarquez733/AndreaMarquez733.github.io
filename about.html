Biography

Who I Am

I'm Andrea Marquez, a junior studying Journalism and Advertising at Texas Tech University's College of Media & Communication. Growing up bilingual in a Spanish-speaking household shaped how I see storytelling as something that must reach people where they are, in the language they live in.

My academic and professional background spans newsrooms, restaurant floors, and recreation center front desks every environment has taught me something different about how people communicate and what they need. Whether I'm writing a news feature or designing a webpage, I lead with empathy and precision.

I'm active in student life at Texas Tech participating in Student Government, KTXT Radio, the Hispanic Student Society, and Women in Communications because I believe in building the communities you want to see. Outside of campus, I'm a certified Serve Safe professional with hands-on experience in fast-paced hospitality settings, where I've learned that real service is about anticipating what people need before they ask.

I'm actively building a portfolio that blends traditional journalism skills with modern digital presentation, and I'm looking for opportunities where both of those worlds intersect.
