Who We Are

We Believe the World Is Better Explored Slowly

Wanderlust Magazine was founded by travel writers who were tired of content that told people where to go without ever explaining why it mattered. We cover places with depth, honesty, and an unapologetic love of getting lost.

We are based everywhere and nowhere — our team works remotely from six countries, which we consider an occupational perk.

Our Values

What Drives Us

🗺️

Depth Over Volume

We publish fewer stories than most travel sites. Every piece we run has been reported in person, fact-checked, and edited with care. We'd rather be good than prolific.

🤝

Responsible Travel

We think hard about the impact of the places we recommend. We flag overtourism, champion local businesses, and try to reckon honestly with travel's environmental costs.

✍️

Real Writing

Our writers are writers first. We care about sentences, about voice, about the kind of storytelling that makes a place feel alive before you've ever been there.

The Author

Meet the Writer

Andrea

Editor & Travel Writer

Andrea is the founder and lead writer of Wanderlust Magazine. With a background in journalism and a passport full of stamps from over 50 countries, she brings both rigorous research and a personal voice to every story she tells.

She specializes in slow travel, cultural immersion, and finding the places that feel untouched by the modern tourism machine. When she's not on the road, she's writing from a small apartment with too many maps on the walls.

Her work has appeared in print and digital publications across Europe and North America. She believes the best travel writing doesn't just describe a place — it makes you feel like you've already been there.
